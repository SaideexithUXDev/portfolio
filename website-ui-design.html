Our Vision

Designing an innovative and user-friendly infotainment system prototype for the Mercedes-Benz G-Class Electric.

Problem Statements

  • Complex Interfaces: Current systems have overly complicated menus, making navigation and usability difficult for drivers.
  • Lack of Personalization: Infotainment systems fail to adapt to individual user preferences, requiring manual adjustments repeatedly.
  • Poor EV Support: Limited focus on EV-specific needs like battery range, charging station locations, and consumption insights.

Research Phase

  • User Needs: Identified the need for quick access to vehicle status and navigation tailored for off-road and electric vehicle users.
  • Competitive Analysis: Studied Tesla and Range Rover systems to pinpoint gaps and opportunities for a luxury electric SUV.
  • Scenarios: Mapped key use cases like off-road navigation and energy management for long trips.
